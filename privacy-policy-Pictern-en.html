Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

Pictern is a macOS application that operates entirely offline. All image processing is performed locally on your device. No data is uploaded to the internet. This privacy policy explains how we protect your privacy.

Information Collection

Pictern does not collect any personal information. All images, conversion results, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Information Usage

Since we do not collect any data, your information is not used in any way. All image processing is performed entirely on your Mac.

Information Sharing

Pictern does not share any information with any third parties. All data remains on your device.

Data Security

Your images and PDF files are stored on your local device, protected by macOS security mechanisms. We recommend keeping your macOS up to date for optimal security.
