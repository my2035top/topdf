Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Batch Image to PDF ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Data Collection

Batch Image to PDF does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All image processing and PDF conversion is performed entirely on your device. No data is ever sent to external servers.

2. Local Processing

The App operates fully offline. Your images and the resulting PDF files remain exclusively on your Mac. The App does not have internet access and does not communicate with any remote services.

3. User Preferences

The App saves your language preference and conversion settings locally on your device using macOS standard storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your computer.

4. Log Files

The App maintains a local conversion log for your convenience. This log is stored only on your device and can be cleared at any time. It is never transmitted externally.

5. Third-Party Services

Batch Image to PDF does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third-party SDKs are used.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
