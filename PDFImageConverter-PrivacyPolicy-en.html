Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Summary: PDF Image Converter does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs 100% locally on your device and never connects to any network.

1. Data Collection

PDF Image Converter does not collect, gather, or record any personal information, user data, or usage statistics. The application operates entirely offline.

2. Data Transmission

The application does not have any network functionality. No data is ever sent from your device to any server, including:

  • No telemetry or usage analytics
  • No crash reports sent to third parties
  • No user accounts or authentication
  • No cloud storage or synchronization

3. Local File Processing

All PDF-to-image conversion is performed locally on your Mac using Apple's built-in PDFKit framework. Your PDF files and converted images remain exclusively on your device. No files are uploaded, copied, or transmitted to any remote server.

4. Third-Party Services

PDF Image Converter does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data.

5. Permissions

The app only requests access to files that you explicitly select via the macOS file picker dialog. It does not access any files or data beyond what you choose to convert.

6. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, this application does not pose any risk to children's privacy.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
