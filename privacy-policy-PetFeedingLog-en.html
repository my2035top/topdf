Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

Thank you for using Pet Feeding Log (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We deeply value your privacy, and this Privacy Policy clearly and transparently explains how the App handles your information.

🔒 Core Commitment: This App does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data.

1. What Information We Collect

We do not collect any information.

This App has no account registration system, no user login functionality, and does not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. You do not need to provide any personal information to use this App.

2. Permission Usage

This App does not request any system permissions (such as Photos, Camera, Location, etc.). All data is stored solely in your device's local storage.

We will never:

  • Upload your data to any server
  • Share your data with any third party
  • Use your data for any purpose beyond core functionality

All data processing is performed entirely on your device.

3. Data Storage

The pet information and feeding records generated by this App are stored solely in your device's local storage (JSON files in the Documents directory).

This App does not read, scan, upload, or analyze any other files or data on your device.

Deleting the App will simultaneously clear all in-app data.

4. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party services.

Specifically, this App does not include:

  • Third-party advertising SDKs
  • Third-party analytics tools
  • Third-party crash reporting tools
  • Third-party social sharing SDKs
  • Any form of user behavior tracking

5. Data Security

Since this App does not collect any user data:

  • There is no server-side storage of user data
  • There are no security risks during data transmission
  • There is no possibility of data leakage

Your data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's system-level security mechanisms (iOS sandboxing, file encryption).

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not designed for children under 13. Since this App does not collect any personal information, we cannot identify the age of users. If parents or guardians discover that a child is using this App, there is no need to worry about data collection, as this App does not collect any data.

7. Your Rights

Since this App does not collect any personal data, you naturally enjoy complete privacy protection:

  • No need to request data access or export (as no data is collected)
  • No need to request data deletion (as no data is stored on servers)
  • Uninstalling the App will clear all in-app data

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy is changed, we will update it on this page. Since this App does not collect user data, we cannot notify you via email or other means. We recommend checking this page periodically.
