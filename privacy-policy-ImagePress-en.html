Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

Thank you for using MinImagePress (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We deeply respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy clearly and transparently explains how the App handles your information.

🔒 Core Commitment: This App does NOT collect, upload, or share any of your personal data.

1. What Information We Collect

We do not collect any information.

The App has no account registration system, no user login functionality, and does not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. You do not need to provide any personal information to use this App.

2. Permission Usage

The App requests the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

  • Photo Library Read Access: To select images for compression or cropping
  • Photo Library Write Access: To save processed images to your photo album

We will NEVER:

  • Upload your images to any server
  • Share your images with any third party
  • Use your images for any purpose beyond the core functionality

All image processing is performed entirely on your device, with no internet connection required.

3. Data Storage

All data generated by this App is stored exclusively on your device's local storage.

Processed images are saved to your system photo album, under your complete control. App preference settings (such as language and theme) are stored locally in the device's UserDefaults.

The App does not read, scan, upload, or analyze any other files on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party services.

Specifically, this App does NOT contain:

  • Third-party advertising SDKs
  • Third-party analytics tools
  • Third-party crash reporting tools
  • Third-party social sharing SDKs
  • Any form of user behavior tracking

5. Data Security

Since this App does not collect any user data:

  • There is no server-side storage of user data
  • There is no security risk during data transmission
  • There is no possibility of data breaches

Your image data is stored in your device's local photo album, and its security is protected by your device's operating system.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not designed for children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any personal information, we cannot determine the age of users. If parents or guardians discover that children are using the App, there is no need to worry about data collection, as the App does not collect any data.

7. Your Rights

Since this App does not collect any personal data, you naturally enjoy complete privacy protection:

  • No need to request data access or export (as no data is collected)
  • No need to request data deletion (as no data is stored)
  • Uninstalling the App will clear all local settings data within the App

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, we will post the changes on this page. Since the App does not collect user data, we cannot notify you via email or other means. We recommend checking this page periodically.
